# 3. Data Analysis

## Analysis Overview

20 SQL queries and 7 Python EDA charts covering email funnel performance, campaign type comparison, segmentation effectiveness, and engagement timing.

---

## 1. Email Funnel Overview

The standard email marketing funnel measured across all campaigns:

```
Sent → Delivered → Opened → Clicked → Converted → Revenue
```

Key funnel metrics tracked at each stage to identify drop-off points and optimization opportunities.

---

## 2. Campaign Type Performance

Analysis covers performance differences across:
- **Welcome Series** — highest open rates (new subscribers)
- **Promotional** — highest revenue per email
- **Newsletter** — highest volume, moderate engagement
- **Re-engagement** — critical for churn prevention
- **Transactional** — highest conversion rate

---

## 3. Segmentation Analysis

Customer segments analyzed for differential response rates:
- New vs. returning customers
- High-value vs. standard customers
- Engagement-based segments (active, at-risk, churned)

---

## 4. Send Time Optimization

Analysis of open and click rates by:
- Hour of day (morning vs evening peaks)
- Day of week (weekday vs weekend patterns)
- Device type distribution (mobile vs desktop)

---

## Further Exploration

- **A/B Test Analysis:** Subject line length, emoji usage, personalization impact on open rate
- **Churn Prediction:** Model which customers are about to unsubscribe
- **Optimal Send Frequency:** At what frequency do unsubscribes spike?
- **Revenue Attribution Windows:** 1-day vs 7-day vs 30-day click attribution
- **List Health Monitoring:** Track bounce rate trend and clean inactive contacts
